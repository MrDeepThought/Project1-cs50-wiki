# Nike, Inc.



-----



**Nike, Inc.** is an American [multinational corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multinational_corporation) that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. The company is headquartered near Beaverton, Oregon, in the Portland metropolitan area. It is the world's largest supplier of [athletic shoes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sneakers) and [apparel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clothing) and a major manufacturer of sports equipment, with revenue in excess of US$24.1 billion in its fiscal year 2012 (ending May 31, 2012).
